Types of Farms

Learn about different types of farms you can build in Minecraft!

Sugar Cane Farm

A farm for growing sugar cane used for crafting paper, sugar, and trading with villagers.

Wood Farm

Grows trees to harvest wood for building, crafting, and fuel.

Crops Farm

Used for planting and harvesting crops like wheat, carrots, and potatoes for food.

Lava Farm

A farm for creating and collecting lava for use in smelting or crafting.

Stone Farm

Generates stone blocks through lava and water interaction, providing a renewable resource.

Iron Farm

Generates iron ingots by creating and killing iron golems.

String Farm

Creates string by duping it.

XP Farm

Used to gather experience points by killing mobs automatically.

Creeper Farm

Used to spawn and kill creepers, providing gunpowder for TNT and fireworks.

Villager Hall Farm

Generates resources through villager trades, providing a steady supply of goods.

Smelting Room Farm

A smelting system that automatically processes ores and items for you.

Raid Farm

Automatically farms loot from illager raids and provides emeralds and other loot.

Magma Farm

Generates magma blocks in the Nether, providing magma for crafting or building.

Gravity Block Farm

Uses falling sand, gravel, or other gravity-affected blocks to generate resources.

Ender Man Farm

A farm that spawns Endermen to generate Ender Pearls and experience.
